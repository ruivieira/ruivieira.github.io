Pikchr

Updated 2022-06-26 (3b40f94)

Notes on Pikchr1.

Installation

Download pikchr~ from the downloads page To create the CLI command, compile using

$ gcc -DPIKCHR_SHELL -o pikchr pikchr.c -lm

And add it to your path.

Examples

A: box "head" fit
B: box "tail" fit
C: box "something" with .sw at A.nw fit wid dist(A.w, B.e)
